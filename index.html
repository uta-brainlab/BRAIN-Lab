Welcome to BRAINAI@UTA

The Brain and AI Research Lab at the University of Texas at Arlington, led by Dr. Xi Zhu, focuses on developing computational approaches to understand the neural mechanisms underlying cognitive processes and psychiatric disorders. Our interdisciplinary research bridges neuroimaging, machine learning, and psychiatry.

Neuroscience

Investigating brain function and connectivity through neuroimaging techniques

Machine Learning

Developing computational models to analyze complex neural data patterns

Mental Health

Translating research insights into improved understanding of psychiatric disorders
