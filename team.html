Our Team

Meet the researchers and students behind BRAIN Lab @ UTA

Lab Team Members

Xi Zhu, Ph.D.

Principal Investigator

Dr. Xi Zhu is an Assistant Professor at the University of Texas at Arlington. Her research focuses on the development of computational methods to understand the neural mechanisms underlying cognitive processes and psychiatric disorders. Previously, she was with Columbia University's Department of Psychiatry where she conducted research on computational psychiatry and neuroimaging methods.

Join Our Team

We are always looking for motivated individuals who are passionate about computational approaches to understanding the brain and mental health.

Graduate Students

Interested in pursuing a Ph.D. or M.S. with our lab? Please contact Dr. Zhu with your CV and a brief statement of research interests.

Undergraduate Students

We welcome UTA undergraduate students interested in gaining research experience in computational neuroscience and psychiatry. No prior research experience is required, but programming skills (Python, R, or MATLAB) are beneficial.

Postdoctoral Fellows

We occasionally have openings for postdoctoral researchers. If you're interested, please contact Dr. Zhu with your CV, research statement, and representative publications.
