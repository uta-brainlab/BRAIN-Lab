Explainable AI for PTSD Biotypes

This project focuses on using Explainable AI (XAI) approaches to identify distinct PTSD biotypes and develop personalized treatment strategies. By making AI decision-making processes transparent and interpretable, we aim to enhance our understanding of PTSD subtypes and improve treatment outcomes.

Key Research Questions

  • How can XAI help identify distinct PTSD biotypes?
  • What neural mechanisms differentiate PTSD subtypes?
  • How can explainable models inform treatment selection?
  • What are the key predictors of treatment response?

Methodology

We employ advanced XAI techniques alongside neuroimaging analysis to develop interpretable models that can identify PTSD subtypes and predict treatment outcomes. Our approach emphasizes both model performance and clinical interpretability.

Current Status

We are developing and validating XAI models using multimodal data, including neuroimaging, clinical assessments, and behavioral measures, to identify robust PTSD biotypes and their associated treatment responses.
