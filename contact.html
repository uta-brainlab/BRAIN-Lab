Join Our Team

We're always looking for talented and motivated individuals to join our research team. Below are current opportunities and information for prospective lab members.

Graduate Students

We welcome applications from prospective PhD and Master's students interested in computational psychiatry, neuroimaging, and machine learning applications in mental health research.

Qualifications:

  • Strong background in psychology, neuroscience, computer science, or a related field
  • Programming experience (Python, R, MATLAB, etc.)
  • Interest in interdisciplinary research at the intersection of brain science and AI

Interested applicants should first apply to the relevant graduate program at UTA and indicate Dr. Xi Zhu as a potential advisor. You may also email Dr. Zhu directly with your CV, research statement, and transcripts.

Postdoctoral Fellows

We occasionally have openings for postdoctoral researchers with expertise in computational neuroscience, machine learning for healthcare, or psychiatric neuroimaging.

Current Opportunities:

  • Computational Psychiatry Postdoc: Position focused on developing machine learning approaches for predicting treatment response in mood disorders using multimodal data.

To apply, please send your CV, research statement, and contact information for three references to xi.zhu@uta.edu with the subject line "Postdoc Application".

Undergraduate Research Assistants

Undergraduate students at UTA interested in gaining research experience are encouraged to apply for research assistant positions in our lab.

Requirements:

  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Commitment of at least 8-10 hours per week
  • Interest in research in neuroscience, psychology, or computer science

Please fill out our undergraduate research application form if you're interested in joining the lab.
